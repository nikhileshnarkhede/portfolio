About Me

I'm a Data Science graduate student at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth (4.0 GPA), specializing in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI applications for engineering and materials science.

My research focuses on physics-aware machine learning frameworks, symbolic regression analysis, and deep neural networks for predicting mechanical properties of advanced materials. I've published in the Journal of Composite Science and actively work on cutting-edge additive manufacturing optimization.

Previously, I interned at DRDO (India's Defense R&D) and Bajaj Auto, applying mathematical modeling, CFD simulations, and predictive maintenance to real-world engineering challenges.
