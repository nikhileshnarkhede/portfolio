Publications

Latest · 2026

Hybrid Machine Learning Workflow for Process-Property Optimization in Fused Filament Fabrication

Int. Journal on Interactive Design and Manufacturing (IJIDeM), Springer · 2026

A hybrid, interpretable pipeline coupling symbolic regression (gplearn) with a DNN surrogate and NSGA-II multi-objective optimization to map FFF process parameters to six mechanical properties of ABS. The DNN refines the white-box symbolic features, lifting R² to 0.96–0.997 across all properties; NSGA-II then recovers Pareto-optimal print settings validated against experiment.
